Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about Rosnet Academy

What classes and subjects do you offer coaching for?
We offer coaching for standard school classes (Primary to Higher Secondary) across all major boards (CBSE, State Board, ICSE). Our core subjects include Mathematics, Physics, Chemistry, Biology, and Computer Science.
Do you provide personalized attention for slow learners?
Yes, personalization is at our core. We maintain a healthy student-to-teacher ratio and offer special remedial sessions for students who need extra support in specific topics.
Are online classes available?
Yes, we provide high-quality interactive online coaching via modern digital platforms, alongside our traditional physical classroom sessions in Coimbatore.
How do you track student progress?
We conduct regular weekly assessments and monthly grand tests. Detailed progress reports are shared with parents during our monthly PTM (Parent-Teacher Meetings).
