Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about Rosnet Academy

What classes do you offer coaching for?
We offer comprehensive coaching for students from 1st to 12th Standard. This includes all major boards such as CBSE, ICSE, and State Board (Samacheer Kalvi). We also have specialized batches for college-level subjects and competitive exams.
What are the typical academy timings?
Our academy is open from Monday to Saturday, 9:00 AM to 8:30 PM. We offer multiple batches to accommodate different school timings:
  • Evening Batches: 4:30 PM - 6:30 PM & 6:30 PM - 8:30 PM
  • Morning Batches (During Exams): 6:00 AM - 8:00 AM
  • Special Weekend Sessions for Board Exam students.
Do you provide study materials?
Yes! Every student receives curated study materials, simplified notes for complex topics, and a repository of previous years' question papers. We also provide our signature "Exam Strategy Kits" for 10th and 12th-grade students.
How do you track and report student progress?
At Rosnet Academy, we leverage technology to ensure complete transparency in every student's learning journey. We conduct comprehensive assessment tests every Sunday to evaluate weekly progress. All performance data is instantly updated on our dedicated Rosnet Academy App, available on the Google Play Store.
Is there an option for online coaching?
Absolutely. We offer "Rosnet Hybrid," which allows students to attend sessions remotely via our interactive virtual classroom. This is ideal for students residing outside Coimbatore or those who prefer studying from home.
